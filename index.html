Grapes Club is India’s 1st Integrated Technology Platform for the Disabled Community.

We aim to provide a social Networking platform for the disabled community wherein they can create “community within community” and enjoy feeling of togetherness via chat, message, videos, post and share their experience with each other similar to a saying “Birds of Feather Flock together”. We are using best of the technology (Talkback, Voice Access etc.) so that every sector of the disabled community can use our platform without any hurdles.







Unique Mobile App

Now connect using extensively tested, secure & easy to use app, irrespective of the disability (Visual Impairment, Hand Amputees, Paralysed and others), embedded with best of the technology, available on both iOS and Android.
